WISE Chapters

Our chapters are spread across major cities and regions, providing local networking and professional development opportunities for women in supply chain management.

Northeast Chapter

Location: New York, NY

Founded: 2015

Members: 150+

Serving the New York metropolitan area with monthly networking events and quarterly workshops.

Midwest Chapter

Location: Chicago, IL

Founded: 2016

Members: 120+

Connecting supply chain professionals in the Midwest with industry insights and career advancement programs.

Texas Chapter

Location: Houston, TX

Founded: 2017

Members: 180+

The largest chapter, focused on logistics and port management with regular industry partnerships.

West Coast Chapter

Location: Los Angeles, CA

Founded: 2018

Members: 140+

Connecting women in supply chain across California, focusing on innovation and sustainability.

Southeast Chapter

Location: Atlanta, GA

Founded: 2019

Members: 95+

Growing chapter dedicated to professional development and mentorship in the Southeast region.

Pacific Northwest Chapter

Location: Seattle, WA

Founded: 2020

Members: 85+

Newest chapter focusing on technology and digital transformation in supply chain.
